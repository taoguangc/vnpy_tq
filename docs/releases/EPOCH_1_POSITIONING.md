# Epoch 1 Positioning — Evidence-Centric Research OS

**Status:** Active positioning（governance narrative；not Decision / Spec / ABR）  
**Checkpoint:** Epoch 1 Midpoint Stable Checkpoint  
**Audience:** Future contributors（read this before code）

## Paradigm shift

| Era | Orientation | First-layer output |
|-----|-------------|--------------------|
| AFF / early | **Strategy-centric** | Profit? |
| Epoch 1 | **Evidence-centric** | Knowledge |

AFF-shaped loop（rejected as primary）:

```text
Hypothesis → Strategy → Backtest → Profit?
```

Epoch 1 loop（authoritative）:

```text
Hypothesis → Experiment → Evidence → Evaluation → Validation → Knowledge
```

**Profit is not a first-layer output.**  
Profit is a downstream consumer of Knowledge.

## PAAF Research Loop

```text
Question
      ↓
Hypothesis
      ↓
Experiment
      ↓
Evidence
      ↓
Evaluation
      ↓
Validation
      ↓
Knowledge
      ↓
Next Question
```

Alpha / Detector / OPP / Market State are **Hypothesis forms**, not the stable core.  
The loop is the stable core.

## Primary asset

Not Repository, Projection, or Validation alone.

> **Research Governance** is PAAF’s DNA.

Any research path must pass:

```text
Need → Experiment → Evidence → Validation
```

Forbidden shortcut:

```text
Idea → Backtest profit → Production
```

Code may be rewritten. Baselines may evolve.  
Governance that forces Evidence before belief is what must survive.

## How to read v0.4（when Consider becomes Need）

Roadmap name: Validation Protocol.  
Deeper meaning: **Research Protocol** — what experiments are worth running, what Evidence is enough, what may promote, what must HOLD.

Validation is one step inside that protocol — not a license to build another analysis engine.

Do **not** Approve v0.4 until a real research Need demands it.

## When Epoch 1 ends

Not “now.”  
Not “when profitable.”

Epoch 1 ends when the first real Alpha candidate completes the full governance chain:

```text
Hypothesis → Experiment → Evidence → Validation → Promotion → Production Candidate
```

under Epoch 1 rules — regardless of whether PnL looks attractive.

That day Architecture becomes legacy DNA; Research productivity becomes the measured output.

## One-line positioning

> **PAAF is not a project for finding trading strategies; it is a research operating system for continuously producing trustworthy trading knowledge.**
